Geek and Sundry

Geek and Sundry is commercial YouTube channel and multimedia production hub that focuses on brodcasting tabletop RPGs, boardgames, card games and other beautiful nerdy shenanigans. I particularly enjoy Critical Role; in which a group of our favorite nerdy professional voice actors gather a play Dungeons & Dragons

Heroforge

Heroforge is a source that allows you to build and order customized mini figures to use for your campaign

Roll20

Having trouble finding a group of people to play with.... yeah it can be a tough go... these situations can be pretty familiar



but Roll 20 is a virtual tabletop that enables players and DMs alike to look for groups, post campaign contents, and even just have a discussion about the latest edition in the forums
